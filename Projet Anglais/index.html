Your Name is film is a Japanese romantic animated fantasy film produced by CoMix Wave Film and released by Toho in 2016. It talks about 2 teenagers seeking to see each other, while discovering that a meteor is coming soon and will hit the earth very soon.

The animated film was one of the most popular anime ever, and in this website we will show you all the informations you need to know. And even some places you can visit if your plan to go on a trip to Japan to see the places the movie was inspired.

SUGA SHRINE – TOKYO

The Suga Shrine was used for the very last scene when Mitsuha and Taki pass each other on the stairs. It is located in Tokyo.

This is where the final scene takes place, where the two characters meet again and remember their names

Hie-Jina Shrine - TAKAYAMA

the shrine where Mitsuha prepares Kuchikamizake, the sacred alcohol for the Gods. The model is in fact the Hie-jinja shrine located in Takayama, in the Gifu prefecture. The Torii is a little bit different but otherwise we find the same atmosphere.

SUWA LAKE – OKAYA

This lake located in Nagato prefecture is the perfect place to take a break and enjoy the beautiful view at night.

Itomori Lake, the main place of the film, is inspired by a real lake which is Suwa Lake, in Nagano Prefecture.
The story of the film in the countryside is supposed to take place in Gifu Prefecture, but Makoto Shinkai, the author of the film, has blurred the lines a little by inserting this lake whose model is in Nagano Prefecture.
Makoto Shinkai being native of Nagano prefecture, he surely wanted to integrate a place of his home.

About The Movie
