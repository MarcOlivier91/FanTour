3 “must see” places close by

Akihabara

It is impossible to be an otaku and not visit THE most popular place for all lovers of manga and anime culture : Akihabara

Located near the Akihabara Station in the Chiyoda ward of Tokyo, it is very known for its mangas shops and cosplay cafes. You can also visit arcade games and sanctuaries near Akihabara.

Suwa Taisha Shimosha Akimiya

The Suwa Taisha, also known as Suwa Grand Shrine is a group of Shinto shrines in Nagano prefecture, Japan. This shrine is considered as one of the oldest shrines in existence, being implied to already stand in the lake 7th century.

Shiroyama Park

The Shiroyama Park is a public park located in Gifu Prefecture, Japan. This park is established in the mountainous area, around the ruins of Takayama Castle.
